Madrid

5 Day Experience

€495 / person

Learn about Spanish wine and discover traditional Iberian ham houses in Madrid. During this tour, taste several cuts of Iberian meats like chorizo, salchichon, lomo, and ham paired with manchego cheese and other classic tapas. As you do so, learn about the fascinating history and culture of wine, the differences between each type of ham you can find in the market and master how to shop for them like a local.

  • Sample Spain's best Iberian ham, wine and tapas during this Madrid food tour
  • Taste high-end ham specialty products like salchichón, lomo and chorizo
  • Learn about differences in production between different styles of ham like Jamón Serrano and Jamón Iberico
  • Enjoy delicious Spanish cheeses, wines and tapas to accompany your ham tasting
  • Learn about Spanish ham production from your expert food guide
